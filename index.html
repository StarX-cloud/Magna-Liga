Rules

General Rules

  • All participants must register with their in-game username.
  • Matches are played in the Head-to-Head (H2H) mode.
  • Using another person’s account is prohibited.
  • Players must schedule and complete matches before the deadline.

Match Rules

  • No use of external tools, exploits, or bugs for an unfair advantage.
  • Intentional quitting or disconnecting mid-match is prohibited.
  • Disconnections in the first half require a restart; otherwise, the game is forfeited.

League Structure

  • Win: 3 points | Draw: 1 point | Loss: 0 points
  • Tiebreakers: Goal difference → Goals scored → Vs Attack (Penalties)

Penalties and Violations

  • Cheating results in a -10 point deduction.
  • No-show results in an automatic 1-0 forfeit.

Additional Rules

  • Players can make up to 3 transfers during the transfer window.
  • Red cards result in a 1-match suspension.
  • Yellow cards in two consecutive matches also result in a suspension.

All league board decisions are final.
